Giants in the technological space are often stuck in a culture of walled gardens instead of open spaces. Their ever-open eyes often focus on gathering as much data as possible from anyone using their services. Their purpose is guided by advertiser's wants.

Unlike these giants, technology like bitcoin, nostr, and web5 are giving back control to the individual. They are opening innovative creative roads built on exchanging value for value.
